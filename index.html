Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, coinciding with the migration of Mexican workers to the US. These workers brought their culinary traditions, including tacos, to the Southwest. While initially seen as street food for the lower class, the taco gained popularity as Mexican immigrants integrated and their economic standing improved.(Source: Twisted Taco the-history-of-Taco)
